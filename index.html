Sunshine Resort

About

Located on the beachfront and direct access to the Golden Golf Club, this family friendly resort is ideal for day time lounging and travellers seeking a private ambience alongside the sea. The grounds is studded with beautiful palmtrees. Sunshine offers restuarants that serve 5-star international cuisine. There are highly personalised services including massages,lake-style pools and suite service. There is is an extensive selection for entertainment programme available to all visitors. There is a romantic gazebo located next to the sea next to the BlueWater Convention Center, making it a perfect option for professional events and ceremonies on the beautiful beaches of Cuba.

Services

Spa

Sunshine Resort offers it's guests superb spa services at The Oasis Spa, a serene oasis of ultimate wellbeing, surrounded by sophisticated luxury. A fabulous world of exotic views and settings combined with the sapphire-blue and warm winds from the sea. Services include massages,facial treatments, beauty parlor and sauna rentals!

Paddleboard Service

Want to get into the water but don't want to get soaked? Take a paddle board lesson in a group or individually! Paddleboard rentals and lessons are available on a per-hour basis. Enjoy the blue waters while soaking up the sun.

Nightly Programming

Want to experience a night full of music and dancing? Nightly coffee houses, concerts, dances and art gatherings are hosted at the Hudson Club House! Enjoy a beach side camp fire with a loved one or friends.
